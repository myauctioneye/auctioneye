Real specs from the US government database. Make, model, engine, drive, fuel — all verified. Same source every dealer uses.
Real profit margin calculator

FAQ

Common questions

Where does the vehicle data come from? +
Vehicle specs come directly from the NHTSA vPIC database — the official US government source. Title history comes from NMVTIS, the national motor vehicle title database.
Does it work with Copart and IAAI? +
Yes. Just grab the VIN from the lot listing on Copart or IAAI. AuctionEye works with any US vehicle VIN regardless of which auction platform you use.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes — cancel anytime with no fees. Your plan stays active until the end of the billing period.
What's included in the free plan? +
5 analyses per month with full VIN lookup, profit margin calculation, risk flags, and the Deal/Neutral/Trap verdict. No credit card required.
How accurate is the market value estimate? +
For free users, the estimate is based on make, model, and year. Pro and Dealer plans also pull real local listings from Craigslist and Facebook Marketplace in your area.
