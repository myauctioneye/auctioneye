FAQ

Common questions

Where does the vehicle data come from? +
Vehicle specs come from the NHTSA vPIC database — the official US government source. Title history comes from NMVTIS, the national motor vehicle title database.
Does it work with Copart and IAAI? +
Yes. Just grab the VIN from the lot listing. AuctionEye works with any US vehicle VIN regardless of which auction platform you use.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes — cancel anytime with no fees. Your plan stays active until the end of the billing period.
What's included in the free plan? +
5 analyses per month with full VIN lookup, profit margin calculation, risk flags, and the Deal/Neutral/Trap verdict. No credit card required.
How accurate is the market value estimate? +
Free plan uses make/model/year estimates. Pro and Dealer plans pull real local listings from Craigslist and Facebook Marketplace near you.
