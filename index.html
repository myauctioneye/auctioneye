FAQ

Common questions

Where does the vehicle data come from? +
Vehicle specs come directly from the NHTSA vPIC database — the official US government source. Every commercial VIN service uses this same data. Title history comes from NMVTIS, the national title database.
Does it work with Copart and IAAI? +
Yes. You just need the VIN number from the lot — you can find it on the Copart or IAAI listing. AuctionEye works with any US vehicle VIN, regardless of which auction platform you use.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes — cancel anytime with no fees. Your plan stays active until the end of the billing period.
What's included in the free plan? +
The free plan includes 5 analyses per month with full VIN lookup, profit margin calculation, risk flags, and the Deal/Neutral/Trap verdict. No credit card required.
How accurate is the market value estimate? +
The estimate is based on make, model, and year — it's a baseline to get you started. For Pro and Dealer plans, we also pull real local listings from your area so you see what comparable vehicles are actually selling for near you.
